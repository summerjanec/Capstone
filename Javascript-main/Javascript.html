ABOUT ME

Why, hello there! I’m Summer Cardozo, a graphic design student at UNLV (for the most part, anyway) who’s pretty much always doodling something. Art has been my favorite way to unwind for as long as I can remember, and I even run a couple of Instagram accounts where I post my drawings. When I’m not sketching or working on class projects, you can usually find me curled up with a good book or rewatching one of my favorite movies (I’m a huge Fantastic Beasts fan, by the way). Thanks for stopping by my page!
